Future Solution

Solution

Our solution will cure the disease by first targeting the mutant PrP proteins with an nanobody, through a process called immunoprecipitation. Then, the particle will allow serine protease to degrade the protein, thereby curing the disease. Our nanoparticle will have a Cu2+ base with an organic coating to ensure proper functioning and ability to cross the blood-brain barrier.

Breakthroughs

Though the success of our design is highly likely, it is important that certain investigations be done first. Nanobodies are already in use today, but it is important that tests be done to ensure that they are safe to use in the brain. Next, serine protease, the key enzyme that will degrade prions in this system, must be tested to ensure that it will not degrade anything but prions. Finally, the base functionality and the safety of using quantum dots and fluorescence in the brain must be understood and tested before our technology can finally come to fruition.
